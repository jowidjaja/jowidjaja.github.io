thoughts.

During my formative years, my consumption of reddit posts was mildly concerning. While I discovered a butt-load of terrible content, I also stumbled upon a selection of well-written articles by genuine people that initiated interesting thoughts. With this blog, I’m here to contribute back to the digital community creating ideas that challenge existing thought patterns and mindsets. Oh, and maybe as place for me to offload the random stuff I think about sometimes (is this just a coping mechanism… haha maybe).

As a disclaimer, I am probably just as confused at the nuances of life as you. Regardless, I think there have been a few helpful things I’ve learned on my journey that certain people at certain stages of life may find beneficial or at least interesting.
